Third Incarnation of Lord Vishnu

Lord Vishnu's third Avatar (incarnation) was in response to the demon Hiranyaksha. At this time, the Earth was rampant with sin, and Hiranyaksha was only thriving in this environment. He started worshipping Lord Brahma to become more powerful.

Impressed with his penance, Lord Brahma came down to bless Hiranyaksha with a boon. The boon he asked for was to not be defeated by any man or God, and a list of animals, but he forgot to mention a boar.

Hiranyaksha carried on tormenting humans on Earth and one day, he abudcted Mother Earth and hid her. This was when Lord Vishnu was asked to intervene and save the Earth. Knowing that the only being he could be defeated by was a boar, Lord Vishnu incarnated as such and challenged Hiranyaksha to a fight. The boar first carried Mother Earth back onto her axis, then fought with Hiranyaksha, ultimately defeating him and restoring balance to the world.
